About our company

Learn more about the company and the team behind it.

The company founders have 15 years accumulative experience in marketing various industrial products in the Philippines. Its service policy has always been to have the local technical service personnel all trained at manufacturer's sites to provide competent local service support to existing customer base.

In February 2003, the Hundredfolds Industries Corp. was officially registered at the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) and with the Philippines Government Electronic Procurement System (PhilGEPS) in June 2006.

At present, Hundredfolds caters to various industrial and medical institutions in the Philippines. It focuses on medical oxygen generator, medical gas distribution system and its accessories, and nitrogen and oxygen generators for both industrial applications.

Hundredfolds also promotes industrial products such as air compressor, compressed air purification equipment and its service, vacuum pumps, mechanical seals, instrumentation equipment, and after-sales service support.

Our Mission

Hundredfolds trust that using innovative and efficient equipment means greater savings for medical and industrial institutions. Thus, in order to help medical and industrial institutions to accumulate bigger revenue, Hundredfolds will source out and introduce state-of-the-art equipment. Also, technical assistance will be provided to maximize the optimum capacity of the equipment.
